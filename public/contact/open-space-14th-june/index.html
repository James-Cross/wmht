Follow up the Full Network session in May

Would you like to talk through the notes, ideas and outcomes of the Network session held with over 100 members at Methodist Central Hall in July? If so, pop into this Open Space.

Event Details:

Tues 14th June

12-1pm

https://us06web.zoom.us/j/81490719028

Lex and Robbie from the GMHAN Co-ordination Group are hosting this Open Space to review the notes which came out of that session and put a bit more flesh on the bones for anyone who wants to go a bit deeper.

No need to register, simply follow this link at 12pm on the 14th June to join in: https://us06web.zoom.us/j/81490719028
